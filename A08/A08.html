A08 Javascript and jQuery FAQ's Assignment

  • FAQ #1: How crazy is the ICS field in UH Manoa?
    All walks of life partake in the journey to achieving the mythical ICS degree here in UH Manoa.
    However, while many enter the ICS field with high hopes and dreams. Only a few make it out with
    the prized degree. While many who enter the UH Manoa campus with hopes and dreams, a true ICS
    student will enter and leave with ways and means.
  • FAQ #2: What does one need to do to acquire this ICS degree?
    The student must first speak to the ICS Elder god, Gerald Lau, for he has the power and ability to allow
    the student embark on their journey. Along their journey, they must find income to provide the items they
    need in order to complete their ICS dream. Items such as a Laptop and software are needed. One does not
    simply attain these items and realize it is easy. One must collect the mystical Monster energy drinks, for
    the elixir it contains allows the student to power through in the darkest of nights and earliest of mornings.
  • FAQ #3: What path should one consider in taking on the ICS field?
    There are many paths a young padawan of the ICS field could go through. Many have chosen the path of the BS route,
    some to the BA route, the IT route, and the bio-informatic route. While there are many roads in life a young ICS padawan
    has before them, one must consider that once a path is chosen that they can never turn back. For it is unwise to invest
    so much time and currency to waste it all and shorten their path mid way. An old, wise student once told his friend this
    message, "just f'ing do it".
  • FAQ #4: What will I encounter in the ICS field?
    The yound padawan will encounter many challenges along their path in finding that ICS degree. It will test their
    limits, patience, faith, endurance, and perseverence. The journey is never an easy one. For one shall endure
    the gates of ICS 111 and 211, where the requirements is a B and above to move on. Many have gone through this gate, yet
    many have decided to end their journey while a few fight through the struggle. The pain has not gone away yet for the
    monster lurking in ICS 311 called Algorithms is awaiting for dinner to arrive and eat upon the hopes and dreams of
    many ICS students. The torturous, painful feeling of lost hope, confusion, anger, and utter sadness comes pressing down
    on the hearts of students who walk through the gates of ICS 311. It is truly where the crossroads of students decide to turn
    away and move onto a newer, different path. However, not all is lost. Once a padawan has moved passed these gates of Hell,
    the light of the heavens shine upon them as they're into the clear and into newer, different pastures of the ICS field.
  • FAQ #5: What will I gain in the ICS field?
    The journey may seem daunting, relentless, and often times depressing. However, a student slowly becomes a journeyman of the
    field and gains immense knowledge during their journey. One will acquire the many known languages of the computer world at their
    disposal, providing themselves the necessary tools given to them by the elder professors. The knowledge of time and efficiency is
    another weapon of choice used at any given notice. The power of talk and manipulation to capture the enemy's data, a dark power taught
    and used for the Hacking Sorcerers. IT Enchantresses are made from the student themselves to provide safety and protection for their party.
    Many who walk the life of an ICS student will leave UH Manoa and hold the power in changing the world, economically, socially, and technologically.
    While the journey is frowned upon and the road less taken, those who endure the journey will be the ones that can say "yeah, we did it."
