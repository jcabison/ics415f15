What is EDC?

EDC or Electrics Daisy Carnival is considered to be one of the biggest massive party events ever to be held in the United States. Hosted by Insomniac Production under Pasquale Rotella, the event has been running strong for almost a decade. Started from small beginnings in California, moving to the Los Angeles Colosseum, and then making its home in the desert of Las Vegas, EDC has been providing the experience of a lifetime for all those familiar to the EDM scene or to anyone who dares to take a leap of faith into the electric sky.
